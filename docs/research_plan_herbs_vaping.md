# Research Plan: Mental Health Benefits of Herbs for Vaping

## Task Overview
Research the mental health benefits of 12 popular herbs for vaping, documenting scientific backing, target conditions, and usage recommendations.

## Target Herbs
1. Ashwagandha
2. Chamomile
3. Lavender
4. Peppermint
5. Holy basil (Tulsi)
6. Ginger
7. Turmeric
8. Rosemary
9. Passionflower
10. Lemon balm
11. Valerian root
12. St. John's wort

## Research Framework
For each herb, document:
- Primary mental health benefits
- Scientific backing/studies
- How vaping enhances absorption
- Target conditions (anxiety, stress, focus, sleep, mood)
- Optimal usage recommendations
